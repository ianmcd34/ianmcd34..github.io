How to play Hurdle

Get from the Start word to the Target word by changing only one letter at a time. You can swap a letter (eg. SPIN->SPUN) , add a letter (eg. SOON->SPOON) or remove a letter (eg. FEATHER->FATHER). You must keep the letters in the same order and the word must be a valid English word held in Hurdle's database. There are three levels of difficulty you may choose, giving you more or less guesses according to Hurdle's algorithm. If the word you enter is in the path that Hurdle has in mind, it will be coloured green. However, it's perfectly possible to get from Start to Target without using any of the words Hurdle has thought of. Good luck!
